Travel is to...

Let your mind go to nature, don't expect to meet anyone, don't expect to encounter any beautiful scenery, imagine yourself to be a free floating person For a small boat in the middle of the lake, the direction can be controlled by the wind or the water, while I just stay quietly. The purpose of traveling is to pack away all the masks and baggage in daily life, go to a strange city, go to a strange environment, or discover all kinds of people, things and scenery; the fun of traveling is that there will be various journeys During the trip, look at the buildings in unfamiliar cities, look at the leisurely old people in different lives, look at different scenery, look at the tranquility of the countryside, the agility of the grasslands, the vastness of the desert, the arrogance of the snow-capped mountains...
